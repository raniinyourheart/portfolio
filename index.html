Book Rental Management System

Book Rental Management System is a web-based application designed to simplify the process of renting books online. The platform enables users to browse available books, rent them for a selected period, complete online payments, and manage the entire rental process digitally. The system also supports return management, overdue tracking, automatic fine calculation, and payment confirmation, providing an efficient and user-friendly experience for both customers and administrators.

Canteen Food Ordering Information System

The Canteen Food Ordering Information System is a web-based self-service ordering platform developed to reduce long queues and improve the dining experience in cafeterias. Customers can browse menus, place orders independently, complete payments through multiple payment methods including QRIS, and receive digital payment receipts. The system also helps restaurant staff manage menus, process orders efficiently, and minimize human errors during daily operations.

Featured Project

DigiTA – Digital Final Project Supervision System

DigiTA is a web-based platform designed to streamline the final project supervision process for university students and lecturers. The application allows students to upload proposal or journal documents, schedule supervision meetings, monitor project progress, and organize tasks using a Kanban board. Supervisors can review, approve, or request revisions, while administrators manage user accounts and supervisor assignments. The platform also provides dashboard and email notifications for supervision schedules, important deadlines, and academic announcements.

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • XAMPP
  • JavaScript

TUNTAS – Nonconformity and Corrective Action Management System

TUNTAS is a web-based organizational governance system developed for Politeknik Negeri Batam to manage nonconformity reports, complaints, suggestions, and corrective actions. The platform digitalizes the reporting workflow by enabling academic members to submit reports, while P4M staff, department heads, and administrators can monitor, review, verify, and track corrective actions efficiently. The system improves transparency, accelerates response times, and maintains a complete history of corrective actions for continuous quality improvement.

QRvent – Multi-Event Digital Guest Book & Smart Check-in Platform

QRvent is a web-based event management platform that modernizes guest registration and attendance tracking using QR Code technology. The platform enables organizers to manage multiple events, perform fast QR-based check-ins, monitor attendance in real time, conduct digital door prize sessions, and collect anonymous participant feedback. Designed with an offline-first architecture and secure token-based access, QRvent ensures reliable operation even during unstable network conditions while reducing paper usage and registration queues.

Featured Project

CarbonTrace – AI Platform for SME Carbon Footprint Management

CarbonTrace is an AI-powered sustainability platform designed to help Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) monitor, analyze, and reduce their carbon emissions in support of Indonesia's Net Zero Emission 2060 initiative. The platform collects operational data such as electricity usage, transportation, production, logistics, and raw materials to calculate carbon emissions (CO₂e). It provides interactive dashboards, AI-powered emission reduction recommendations, sustainability reporting, digital carbon certificates, and collaborative environmental challenges to support greener business operations.

Vaultly – Web-Based Digital Document Management System

Vaultly is a full-stack web application designed to simplify digital document storage and management through a modern and responsive interface. Users can securely upload, organize, search, download, update, and delete files while all file metadata is centrally managed in a relational database. Built with a separated frontend and backend architecture using RESTful APIs, Vaultly provides a scalable, maintainable, and efficient solution for digital file management with support for future enhancements such as cloud storage integration and user authentication.
